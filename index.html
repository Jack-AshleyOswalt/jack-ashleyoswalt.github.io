Jack Ashley-Oswalt

About

Jack Ashley-Oswalt is a guitarist based in Grand Rapids, currently seeking like-minde d musicians who share his commitment to human rights, individuality, and a passion for meaningful political expression. His influences span a wide range of genres, from Rush and Paramore to Chappell Roan. Driven by self-discovery and a dedication to supporting the LGBTQ community, Jack aims to tour and collaborate with musicians who share his vision and values. From an early age, Jack was passionate about music and dreamed of playing guitar. He received his first guitar at age 12 and was instantly hooked. Soon after, he began playing with friends in a group called Meltdown Cinema, gaining valuable experience performing at local festivals, appearing on the local news, and even participating in the PBS program Celebration of Music.
